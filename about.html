Room For More was founded on the belief that possibility should never be limited by proximity or access. Young people of color in Greater Richmond are talented, driven, and full of potential — what they often lack is exposure to the worlds, careers, and opportunities that already exist for them.

We're a 501(c)(3) youth development nonprofit serving students in grades 6 through 12. Through workshops, career exploration experiences, mentorship, and creative development programs, we help young people see beyond what they've been shown — and believe in what they can become.

We don't believe in a single path. There is room for young people of color in classrooms, boardrooms, creative spaces, skilled trades, entrepreneurship, public service, and every future they can imagine. Our job is to make sure they know it.

Mission, Vision & Values

What We Stand For

🎯

Mission

Room For More empowers youth of color in grades 6–12 to realize their full potential by providing access to opportunities, resources, and experiences that expand what they believe is possible — academically, creatively, and professionally.

🌟

Vision

A Greater Richmond where every young person of color has the exposure, support, and access to build the future they deserve — and the belief that they belong in it.

🤝

Approach

We expose, empower, equip, and encourage. Never pity-based, always possibility-based. Youth should feel seen, capable, and supported — not saved.

Access

Breaking down barriers to opportunities, information, and spaces that young people of color deserve to enter.

Exposure

Introducing youth to environments, careers, industries, and pathways they may not otherwise encounter.

Empowerment

Building confidence, self-belief, independence, and the courage to pursue more.

Community

Creating a support system rooted in mentorship, collaboration, guidance, and genuine care.

Possibility

Reinforcing that their dreams are valid, attainable, and worth pursuing — no matter where they're starting from.
